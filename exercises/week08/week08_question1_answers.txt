Questions 1
A) Briefly describe the Strategy Design Pattern?

It defines a family of algorithms and then encapsulates each one which makes them interchangable and allows the algorithm to vary depending on factors such as different users or same task but in a different way.


Gotten from www.sourcemaking.com/design_patterns/strategy


B) When is it appropriate to use the Strategy Design Pattern?

When needing to perform the same task but in different ways or by different clients (users).
