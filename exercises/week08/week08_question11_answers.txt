Question 11
a) What is the Composite Pattern?

A pattern that creates a class which contains a group of its own objects and ways to interact and modify those objects.


b) Under what conditions would you use a Composite Design Pattern?

You would use a composite pattern when you need to treat a group of objects in a similiar way to a single object.


c) What are the four participants of the Composite Design Pattern?

Clents, tree structure, composite objects and the component.
